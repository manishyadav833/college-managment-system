Message from Leadership

Vision • Leadership • Excellence

Rao Bahadur Singh

Founder

Rao Bahadur Singh is the visionary Founder of Yaduvanshi Group, established in 1995 with a mission to provide quality education to rural and semi-urban communities.

His dedication and leadership have transformed thousands of lives. His belief in education as a powerful tool for social and economic growth continues to inspire students to achieve excellence and build a successful future.
